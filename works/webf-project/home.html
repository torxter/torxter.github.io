About the Game

Heroes of the Storm is a 2015 MOBA made by Blizzard. It brings in IP from its various franchises into one big 5v5 arena that focuses on individual character talents and team play. The game is free to play, but has a lot of microtransactions.

This site is generated from my experience of it; it's not an in-depth analysis of the game.

Game Modes

The game has a few different modes to play, including:

  • Quick Match - Choose your preferred character and let the system match you up with teammates and opponents
  • ARAM - Draft from 3 characters on a single lane map.
  • Brawl - Event based mode; recently brought back to the game. Does not follow traditional gameplay.
  • Ranked - Draft, ranked play based by season.
  • Unranked - Draft, unranked play.
  • Custom Games - To play retired maps with custom rules such as preselected characters.
